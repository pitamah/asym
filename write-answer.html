Ecosphere and it's importance

In a simpler way, we are going to explain the ecosphere. It could be considered as if it were a global ecosystem in which all living beings are related to each other directly or indirectly. Let's take the example of photosynthetic organisms. These organisms are responsible for releasing oxygen into the atmosphere and it serves other living beings to feed themselves. The hydrological cycle is also a part of the ecosphere that has relevance throughout the planet. All living beings make use of water since we need it to be able to live.


The process that moves water through the oceans and land is a fundamental phenomenon for life and occurs on a planetary level. This is the hydrological cycle. To take care of the planet we must take care of the ecosphere and take care of ourselves.
